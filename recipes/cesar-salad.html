Cesar Salad

Some Exquisite Cesar salad

Caesar salad is a green salad made with romaine lettuce, croutons, and a creamy, tangy dressing

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • croutons
  • creamy dressing

Steps

  1. Assemble ingredients
  2. Mix lettuce with croutons and finally dressing
  3. Stir and serve!
