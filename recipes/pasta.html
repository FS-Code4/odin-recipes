Pasta

Some delicious Pasta

Here we have some delicious pasta. You can see the mint perfectly lay itself onto the beautifully melted sauce. MMMM, there is nothing like it, just the perfect amount of cheese all to create the perfect salivating bite.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Shells
  • cheese
  • sauce
  • mint

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Drain the pasta
  3. reheat the pasta on low
  4. Add sauce, cheese, and mint
  5. Stir and serve
