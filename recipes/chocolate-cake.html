Chocolate Cake

Perfectly melted chocolate cake

cake flavored with melted chocolate, cocoa powder, or both. It can also have other ingredients such as fudge, vanilla creme, and other sweeteners.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa powder
  • Buttter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix eggs, milk and butter in bowl
  2. Stir until even
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  4. Put on cocoa powder and bake for 45 minutes
